WE GIVE YOU A HOME AWAY FROM HOME

    Welcome to WVO Incomparable Care, Residential Living Home, “Where caring is what we do best”. Here at WVO, you will experience an individualized treatment plan designed to enable you to achieve your maximum level of physical and cognitive function to restore your independence. Our team of professionals include Registered Nurses as well as Personal care assistance, and a Certified assisted living Manager. At WVO, you will find an open door policy to friends and family, professional support, and a warm happy comfortable environment and friendly staff. Our main focus is to treat each resident with the highest quality of care and attention that they rightfully deserve. The staff at WVO are kind and compassionate and carefully screened and selected for the appropriate skills and knowledge, properly trained and closely supervised. Our caregivers have years of experience in residential care homes and caring for elderly with all different medical conditions including Alzheimer’s, Dementia and other aggressive behaviors. At WVO, we are committed to having an open door approach with our residents and families while providing them with a stress free care plan and peace of mind ensuring their loved ones are being cared for. We offer families the opportunity to receive a daily care log of their loved ones care plan online to capture some of the details of the week. We provide electronic video monitoring in all common areas of our family style home. We strive to change the way your loved ones are being cared for.
