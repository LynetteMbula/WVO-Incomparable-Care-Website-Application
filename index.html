WHY US?

WVO is a Residential Living Home Care. A home away from home! Here, we don't,just talk about family, but we walk the walk. We are truly family here.
WVO provides a safe, stable, caring and professional environment in which we provide our clients with programs and activities that inspire them to achieve their highest potential.
We provide for our clients, clinical, physical and mental needs, by exposing them to opportunities that help them to recapture the joy of living.
Our professional team of providers are experienced, dedicated, supportive, compassionate and committed. We pride ourselves on providing a high level of care and support with great attention to detail.
We strive to ensure a stress-free treatment plan that is tailored and personalized to the unique needs of our clients.
We work to foster open communication and a collaborative environment based on trust and partnership between our staff and family members and friends of our community.
